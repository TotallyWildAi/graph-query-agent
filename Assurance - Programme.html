Assist, don't decide
Every flagged case routes to a qualified HSE reviewer who decides assure / return-for-rework. The harness triages and explains with ICAM-layer + evidence rationale — it never closes an investigation. Safety-critical and regulated: human-in-the-loop is mandatory.
